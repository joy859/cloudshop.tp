## 3. Deployment model

- Why does CloudShop choose **public cloud**? (2 benefits, 1 risk)
Benefits:

Lower Cost – CloudShop does not need to buy and maintain expensive servers and hardware. The cloud provider manages the infrastructure.
Scalability – CloudShop can easily increase or decrease resources based on customer demand, especially during peak shopping periods.

Risk:

Security and Privacy Concerns – Data is stored on infrastructure shared with other customers, which may raise concerns about data security and compliance.

- Bank with internal datacenter: which model (public / private / hybrid)?
A bank that operates its own internal datacenter would primarily use a Private Cloud.

Reason:

Banks handle sensitive financial and customer data.
A private cloud provides greater control, security, privacy, and compliance with regulations.
The infrastructure is dedicated to the organization and not shared with other companies.
 Private Cloud.
